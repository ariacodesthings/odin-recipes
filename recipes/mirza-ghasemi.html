Mirza Ghasemi

Mirza Ghasemi is a popular Persian dip made out of tomatoes and eggplants.

Mirza Ghasemi is one of the popular Persian foods, originally from northern Iran. Made with just a handful of ingredients: eggplants, garlic, tomato and tomato paste, oil, eggs, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Roasting eggplants transforms the texture from spongy to silky, and the smoky flavor is paired perfectly with tender sautéed garlic and tomato paste.

Ingredients

  • 5 large eggplants
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste(optional)
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Roast eggplants in the oven, grill or on the stove. Peel eggplants and cut the top off.
  2. Mash eggplants with a fork or finely dice them with a knife.
  3. Mash or finely chop cloves of garlic. Puree tomatoes in blender.
  4. Heat pan and add oil. Sauté the garlic until golden Then add salt, pepper and turmeric.
  5. Add mashed eggplants and cook for 15 minutes on medium heat.
  6. Add pureed tomatoes and tomato paste. Cover and cook for another 15 minutes on medium heat.
  7. Add eggs to the mixture and stir it all together. Cook it for another couple of minutes.
